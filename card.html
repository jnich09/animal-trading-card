Tiger


Every tiger's stripes are unique; similar to a fingerprint.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris.
  • Life span: 10-15 years (in the wild)
  • Speed: Up to 30-40 mph
  • Habitats: Rain forests, grasslands, and savannas

There are nine breeds of tiger: Bengal, Indochinese, Malayan, Siberian, South China, Sumatran, Bali, Caspian and Javan; with the last three being extinct. They can grow up to 11 feet long and weight as much as 670 pounds. Tigers in captivity can live up to 25 years old.
